Customer Returns (Reverse Logistics) – CDS Global performs customer returns processing for both material that we fulfill internally as well as misdirected returns of clients that CDS Global handles their billing activities. We are able to process returns in accordance with client expectations across a large spectrum including destroyal, recycling, grading, or refurbishment of material. We can also prepare material for return shipment to it’s primary fulfillment if needed. Our CDS Global customer care center handles the updating and crediting of customer accounts using all data gathered by our customer returns department. View a map of the facility.
