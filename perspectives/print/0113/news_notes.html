On Nov. 1, 2012, Paul Polus began serving as CDS Global’s chief financial officer. He has a strong history of demonstrated leadership in a diverse range of markets, including seven years in the business process outsourcing industry. He most recently served as CEO and CFO of Cardiff Holdings Corp., and previously held the positions of CFO of card services for Fidelity National Information Services and director of subprime operations analysis for Capital One.

The Audit Bureau of Circulations has changed its name to the Alliance for Audited Media (AAM). The name change reflects the organization’s extension of its auditing services to online and digital content. AAM’s website is now www.auditedmedia.com in the U.S. and www.auditedmedia.ca in Canada.

The new platform offers these benefits:
