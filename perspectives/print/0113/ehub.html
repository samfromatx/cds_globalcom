eHub: Your Digital Entitlement Engine

Introduction

In early 2012, CDS Global launched eHub, our online subscription billing and entitlement management platform that allows companies to build, run and operate subscription and recurring revenue businesses. With eHub, CDS Global is meeting the demands of the digital marketplace. No longer do consumers want to be tied to their print magazine on an issue-by-issue basis; they want access to digital assets from any device at any time. But eHub is for more than just publishers – its capabilities can help customers connect with companies across industries. eHub is available to any business that requires time-based subscription services or recurring payment options to serve their evolving customer needs.

How eHub can Monetize Digital Offerings

eHub has the power to monetize a wide range of digital assets – anything from daily news and special features to crafts, patterns or floor plans – with a platform that offers automatic recurring entitlement to consumers.

Examples include:

  • Paid access to premium digital content
  • Offering access to digital assets such as:
    • Archived assets
    • Crafts and patterns
    • Fitness tools
    • Garden plans
    • Home designs
    • Recipes
    • Woodworking plans

Unique Ways eHub Simplifies the Digital Order Management Process

  • Entitlement: eHub provides consumers with immediate access to digital assets – click here to watch a demonstration.
  • Subscription Amendment: eHub offers a seamless upgrade capability from a monthly product offer to a yearly product offer without interruption in a consumer’s existing entitlement.
  • PCI Compliance: eHub helps companies drastically reduce the scope of their requirements by managing all sensitive credit card data, completely bypassing their internal networks.
  • Reports: eHub provides detailed financial, marketing and support reporting.
  • Integration: A robust set of Application Programming Interfaces (APIs) enables organizations to connect with external entitlement and order channels.
  • Packaged Digital Assets: eHub features the ability to package two or more digital asset products and offer them for one price instead of charging the customer for each product individually (available in early 2013).
  • eTificates: eHub allows customers to gift specific products to a recipient (available in early 2013).

While managing the complexities of your digital orders, CDS Global also provides front- and back-end support to manage customers.
