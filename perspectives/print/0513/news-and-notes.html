CDS Global hosted our Preferred Showcase Tuesday, May 14 from 9:30 a.m. - 5 p.m. EDT on the 44th floor of Hearst Tower. This event provides product overviews and peer-to-peer discussions and promises to be a valuable learning experience for media clients. New this year, workshop sessions allowed publishers to interact, share best practices and learn from one another.

Keynote Speaker: Chris Wilkes, Vice President of Audience Development and Digital Editions for Hearst Magazines – Publishing in the Mobile & Tablet Revolution

Luncheon Speaker: Richard Dorment, Senior Editor, Esquire – Editorial Activities in Multiple Forms of Expression

Large Group Sessions:

  • Fulfillment Company of the Future – The expanded role of the traditional magazine fulfillment company to provide publishers with expertise on services beyond just managing their magazine subscriptions.
  • Publishing in the Mobile & Tablet Revolution (presented by Chris Wilkes, Hearst) – Learn how to effectively monetize content and drive success in digital with logical moves for publishers in the mobile and tablet space, while setting expectations for the challenges ahead in driving new streams of revenue.
  • Improving the Consumer Experience – CDS Global’s view of today’s consumer lifecycle from point of acquisition to creating affinity.
  • Evolution in the Marketplace – Operating in today’s digital marketplace doesn’t have to be a revolution. It can be an evolution. This session will highlight ways CDS Global can help publishers operate in today’s digital and consumer-driven marketplace.
  • Data-driven Customer Insights – The significance of big data, and a proof of concept for CDS Global’s Customer Insights solution, which delivers a single view of the customer.

Interactive Workshops:

  • Leveraging Consolidated Newsstand Data to Make Business Decisions – How publishers can make better sense of their digital newsstand data.
  • Keys to Success in Mobile Optimization and Tablet Support – Ways publishers can easily ensure their pages are mobile-optimized and also support the evolving mobile and tablet consumer.
  • Generate New Revenue with Targeted Advertising – How targeted advertising can prove to be a successful and uncovered source of revenue for publishers.
  • Prospect Insights: How Data Can Fuel Your Business – How publishers can better understand their customers and acquire new ones with additional sources of data.
  • Strategies for Improving Direct Mail Response Rates – Proven strategies and best practices for making better use of direct mail programs that will increase their effectiveness and ROI.
  • Simplifying the Online Consumer Experience – How publishers can improve the online consumer experience with single account logins.
