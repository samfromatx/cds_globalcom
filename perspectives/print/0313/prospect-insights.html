Prospect Insights: A Streamlined Source for Predictive and Targeted Marketing

Organizations have historically purchased volumes of consumer names and relied on approximations to market to potential clients. CDS Global, leveraging more than 40 years of experience in list and data management, has developed a solution that takes the guesswork out of targeting those prospects most likely to engage.

CDS Global’s Prospect Insights offers you the ability to enhance the value of your own data, better understand existing customers, develop a pool of ideal prospects and target those consumers most likely to purchase. Prospect Insights provides a full data acquisition service comprised of acquisition lists, customer profiles and predictive response modeling.

A STREAMLINED THREE-FOLD SOLUTION


Acquisition Lists
If you know what your current customer base looks like, CDS Global can provide a list of prospects based on up to 500 attributes, allowing you to target prospects that mirror your best current customers.

EXAMPLE: A magazine is running an acquisition campaign and knows its target market is women 65-84 years old who live in Minnesota, Wisconsin, Idaho or Utah. CDS Global generates and provides a list of prospects with those same attributes.

Customer Profiles
If you want deeper insights into your customer base, CDS Global can analyze your consumer data and provide a clear picture of your typical customer. From age to geography to home value, leverage known characteristics of existing customers to market to prospects with similar qualities.

EXAMPLE: A publication asks CDS Global to help it better understand its subscribers. CDS Global overlays the publication’s customer data stored on CDS Global’s order management system with demographic and interest attributes, analyzes the data and provides a detailed picture of their current customer base. Knowing what their current customers look like, the publication uses the known attributes to purchase more targeted prospects.

Predictive Response Modeling
Through analytics such as profiling, marketers can develop customer summaries based on demographics, geography, source and tenure. By combining these characteristics with more in-depth modeling of customers, targeted prospects, acquisition efforts, campaign results and individual prospect responses, you can develop a much more detailed picture of individual customers and their tendencies, behaviors and response propensities.

The parameters derived from predictive modeling can then be applied against over 220 million U.S. consumers, who are ranked based on their likelihood to accept a brand’s given offer. By scoring prospects based on highly specific variables and characteristics, you can much more effectively and specifically promote to those candidates most likely to become customers.

EXAMPLE: A publication asks CDS Global to find the 50,000 people most likely to purchase from its brand.
CDS Global appends demographic, interest and behavioral attributes onto the publication’s campaign and customer files, analyzes the data and uses an algorithm to score the entire pool of American consumers based on individuals’ likelihood to respond to an offer. CDS Global presents the publication with the 50,000 individuals from the database with the highest scores – or greatest likelihood to buy.
