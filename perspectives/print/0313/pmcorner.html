Product Management Corner

Personalized Onsert Package Offers a Wide Range of Customizations – Drive 1:1 marketing efforts using our Personalized Onsert Package, available through CDS Global’s Cross-Media Communications. This solution gives your advertisers the ability to customize onserts using known data elements such as name and address, to drive variability and personalization.

Advertisers are able to reach their customers on a more personalized level through uniquely pertinent ads.




 

Transparent Redirect Enhancement with eHub Eliminates Handling and Processing of Credit Card Data from Your Server Environment – This enhancement increases your security and reduces the scope of your PCI compliance. You maintain control of the acquisition channel, keep the same look and feel of a normal checkout process and oversee the consumer shopping experience, but the credit card data is processed and handled outside of your server.

Engage Admin Tool Updates Simplify Processes and Save Time – Recent enhancements include the ability to archive pages and allow outbound promotions to be copied from one title to another under a publisher set. For a complete list of updates, visit the Engage Forum on myCDSGlobal.

Circulation Model Enhancement Provides More Robust Modeling Capabilities – The latest Circulation Model enhancement provides more robust modeling capabilities around your cash and credit card business – important for those with auto-renewal and digital sales. Additionally, this expansion to "Closed Issue Balancing" includes options for arrears, adjustments and extensions. Please see the January Product Management Corner for a full list of enhancements.
