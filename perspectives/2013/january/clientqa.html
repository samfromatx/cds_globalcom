Perspectives Client Q&A

With Monica Ray, Executive Vice President of Consumer Marketing at Condé Nast

Q: What was your career path to your current job?
A: I started as a summer intern at Time Inc. I immediately loved publishing and have only worked
for Time Inc. and Condé Nast since (and that internship was quite a while ago).

Q: How do you measure success?
A: Professionally, it is whether my team is driving results and whether people want to work
on my team.

Q: What are your hidden talents?
A: I love to do old-school craft things such as baking, needlework and botanical drawing.

Q: If you had a crystal ball that could tell you one thing about the future, what would
you want to know?
A: That would be cheating! 

Q: What are some of the most recent books you read?
A:The last two books I read were In Sunlight and In Shadow by Mark Helprin and
The Twelve by Justin Cronin.
