Product Management Corner

Available Now:

Enhancements Made to Web Service Gateway for Demographic History Data – An enhancement to the Web Service Gateway and the SERV Order Management System allows you to make API calls to retrieve not only current demographic data, but also historical information so you can see the evolution of your customers’ activity with a single inquiry.

Trigger Marketing Solution Simplifies Promotions – CDS Global’s Trigger Marketing solution allows you to set up a campaign that promotes offers of other relevant titles in your brand to your most recent subscribers. Hearst Magazines recently implemented this solution and has seen a 10-11 percent average response rate, compared to the typical 2-3.4 percent found in traditional direct mail campaigns.

Engage e-Promos Provides Targeted and Segmented Email Audiences – This product is available for your promotional email marketing campaigns, providing you the ability to reach your current and potential customers. e-Promos allows you to target and segment your prospects and customers, so you can fine-tune your messages based on a target audience to help you drive sales and retention. Our e-Promos solution is flexible – you can use CDS Global resources for design, testing and deployment with data pulled from the fulfillment system, or you can use internal resources and/or a third-party mailing list.

Cross-Media Communications Monetizes Cover Artwork – One of the many ways that you can take advantage of our custom digital printing solution is to repurpose your cover artwork. Cross-Media Communications can produce high-quality posters of your covers that can be purchased through an existing Web store or a simple-to-create storefront.

Electronic Bill Presentment and Payment Enhancements Make Payments Easier

  • Flexible Payments Scheduling: Allows your end users the ability to schedule and edit a range of payments any day of the week and make a payment at any interval, for any amount, when it is convenient for them.
  • Flexible Recurring Payments: Enables your customers to set up recurring payments for a fixed or billed amount at any frequency or interval they choose. Customers may also choose an end date. These payments can be scheduled seven days a week.
  • Mobile Web: Provides the option to have bill presentment available on your mobile Web pages, in addition to the already-available bill payments. You can send your customers a text and/or email that lets them know their bill is available.

Circulation Model Enhancements Will Provide More Robust Modeling Capabilities
Upcoming Circulation Modeling enhancements include:

  • New area for credit card and cash cancel rates and their corresponding distribution patterns will provide you with increased modeling capabilities that are vital to your auto-renewals and ever-increasing digital circulation.
  • When you incorporate the historical data from the InFOCUS Circ+ R720-000 – or the Cash/Credit Card Cancel Timing Flow report – into the new CDS Global Model section, the resulting output will reflect a calculation of your cash/credit card cancels and rejects, providing you with a more definitive budget and forecast.
  • A new MARS report, M305 – or the Credit Card/Cash Subscription Cancellation Analysis – will provide you with detailed descriptions of the new CDS Global Model calculations.
  • The ability to include Arrears/Special Arrears, Adjustments/Extensions as well as other copies from the InFOCUS Circ+ H237-T00 – or the Earned Income by Model Source Report data – will be included in the Closed Issue Balancing Report. This report provides you with a more comprehensive view of your Model calculations in comparison to your closed issues.

Prospect Database Will Target Potential Customers
CDS Global’s Prospect Database will profile your existing clients, then find targeted prospects bearing the same attributes. Generic prospect data, profile-driven data and scored prospect data will be available. Our scored data will be generated by developing predictive models from your customer data and then applying those results to find our lists, resulting in a more targeted campaign and higher response rates.
